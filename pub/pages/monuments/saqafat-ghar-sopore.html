Saqafat Ghar Sopore

Cultural Center - Preserving and Promoting Kashmiri Heritage

About Saqafat Ghar

Saqafat Ghar, meaning "House of Culture," is a cultural center located in the historic town of Sopore in North Kashmir's Baramulla district. This institution serves as a vibrant hub for preserving, promoting, and celebrating Kashmiri culture, arts, literature, and traditional crafts.

The center plays a crucial role in keeping alive the rich cultural traditions of Kashmir through various programs, exhibitions, and cultural events. It provides a platform for local artists, craftsmen, writers, and performers to showcase their talents and connect with the community, making it an important institution for cultural preservation in the region.

Activities & Programs

Art Exhibitions

Regular exhibitions showcasing traditional and contemporary Kashmiri art, including miniature paintings, calligraphy, paper mache, and local handicrafts by talented artists from across Kashmir.

Literary Events

Hosts literary gatherings, poetry recitals (mushaira), book launches, and discussions on Kashmiri literature in Urdu, Kashmiri, and other languages, promoting regional literary heritage.

Cultural Programs

Organizes traditional music performances, Sufi gatherings, folk dance presentations, and other cultural programs that celebrate Kashmir's rich performing arts traditions.

Craft Workshops

Conducts workshops on traditional Kashmiri crafts including carpet weaving, embroidery, walnut wood carving, and paper mache, helping preserve these ancient skills.

Educational Programs

Offers cultural education programs for students and youth, teaching them about Kashmir's heritage, traditions, and the importance of cultural preservation.

Heritage Documentation

Undertakes documentation of local heritage, oral histories, and traditional practices, creating an archive of North Kashmir's cultural wealth for future generations.

About Sopore

Sopore, known as the "Apple Town of Kashmir," is a historic town in Baramulla district, famous for its apple orchards, beautiful landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. The town has been an important center of trade and culture in North Kashmir for centuries.

Located on the banks of River Jhelum, Sopore is surrounded by lush apple orchards and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains. The town is known for its traditional architecture, bustling markets, and warm hospitality, making it an interesting destination for cultural tourism.
