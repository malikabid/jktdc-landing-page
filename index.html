About Directorate of Tourism Kashmir

The Directorate of Tourism Kashmir is the principal government agency responsible for promoting and regulating tourism in the Kashmir Valley. Our mandate encompasses the development of tourism infrastructure, formulation of tourism policies, and showcasing Kashmir's rich cultural heritage and natural beauty to the world.

From the snow-capped peaks of Gulmarg to the tranquil houseboats of Dal Lake, from the Mughal gardens of Srinagar to the pristine meadows of Pahalgam, we work tirelessly to preserve Kashmir's unique identity while ensuring sustainable tourism development that benefits local communities and provides visitors with authentic, memorable experiences in this paradise on earth.
