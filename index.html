Featured Destinations

Srinagar - The Summer Capital

The jewel of Kashmir, famous for the tranquil Dal Lake, iconic Shikaras, and historic Mughal Gardens like Nishat and Shalimar Bagh.

View City Guide

Gulmarg - Meadow of Flowers

A premier skiing destination boasting the Gulmarg Gondola, the world's second-highest operating cable car, surrounded by snowy peaks.

Plan Adventure

Pahalgam - Valley of Shepherds

A serene town located on the banks of the Lidder River, serving as the base camp for the holy Amarnath Yatra and stunning treks.

A Spectrum of Experiences

Adventure & Sports

Skiing, Trekking (KGL, Tarsar Marsar), White-Water Rafting on the Lidder River, and Golf at the highest course in the world.

Culture & Cuisine

Experience the warmth of Kashmiri hospitality, the ancient art of Pashmina weaving, and the elaborate Wazwan multi-course feast.

Pilgrimage & Faith

Information for the sacred Shri Amarnath Ji Yatra, as well as the Hazratbal Shrine and the ancient Shankaracharya Temple.
